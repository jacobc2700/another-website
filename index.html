About Me

My name is Jacob Chang and I am a senior in high school who enjoys computer programming. I spend my free time developing web applications and learning new technologies. Besides programming, I also enjoy playing the piano, solving puzzles, and making videos.

I am currently taking online courses in web development and data science, and I hope to use these skills to develop three large-scale projects with JavaScript, Java, and Python. I am also working on designing my own personal website.
